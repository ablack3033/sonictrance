🌀 SonicTrance

Real-time music visualization, entirely in your browser.

  • 🖥️ Capture Tab / System — visualize whatever is already playing (Spotify web, YouTube…). Pick a tab or screen and check “Share audio”. Chrome & Edge.
  • 🎙️ Microphone — picks up any sound in the room. Works in every browser.
  • 🎵 Open File — play an MP3/FLAC/WAV from your machine.

Keys: 1–4 modes · C theme · F fullscreen · H hide UI

Audio never leaves your machine — everything is analyzed locally.
